# My Favorite Series: Money Heist: Korea – Joint Economic Area

![Money Heist Korea](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/8/80/Money_Heist_Korea_-_Joint_Economic_Area.jpeg)

**Money Heist: Korea – Joint Economic Area** is a thrilling South Korean adaptation of the popular Spanish series *La Casa de Papel*. Set in a reunified Korea, it follows a mastermind known as "The Professor" who assembles a group of skilled criminals to carry out a daring heist on the Korean Unified Mint.

## Why I Love It
- Smart and strategic storyline
- Unique Korean cultural spin on a global hit
- Intense action and emotional depth
- Strong character development

## Main Characters
1. The Professor (Yoo Ji-tae)
2. Tokyo (Jeon Jong-seo)
3. Berlin (Park Hae-soo)
4. Nairobi (Jang Yoon-ju)
5. Rio (Lee Hyun-woo)

## Memorable Quote
> “What do you think a robber is? Someone who steals money with force? No, it's someone who steals time from the system.”

## Fun Fact
The Korean version adds a political twist by imagining a future where North and South Korea are reunified.
